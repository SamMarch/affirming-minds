About Us

At Affirming Minds, we believe in a different approach to mental health care. Our practice is built on the foundations of equity, human rights, and authentic understanding.

We offer evidence-based, heart-centered psychological support that truly understands neurodivergent and LGBTQIA+ experiences from the inside. This isn't just about being "friendly" or "accepting" – it's about deeply understanding the unique challenges, strengths, and perspectives that come with these identities.

Our telehealth-only model means you can access quality psychological care from the comfort of your own space, anywhere in Australia. No fluorescent lights, no waiting rooms, no sensory overwhelm – just genuine support in an environment where you feel safe.

Our Values

  • Client-led care: You are the expert on your own life
  • Neuro-affirming: Celebrating neurodiversity, not trying to "fix" it
  • LGBTQIA+ affirmative: Genuine understanding and celebration of all identities
  • Evidence-based: Using approaches that actually work
  • Accessible: Breaking down barriers to mental health care
  • Collaborative: Working together as equals

How Can We Help?

Finding Your Calm

"Sometimes the bravest thing you can do is ask for support"

Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, or life's overwhelming moments, we're here to help you find your way through with compassion and practical strategies that actually work for your brain.

Understanding Yourself

"Understanding yourself is the first step to thriving"

Discovering you're neurodivergent can be life-changing. We provide support through diagnosis, self-discovery, and learning to work with your brain instead of against it.

Being Your Authentic Self

"Your authentic self deserves to be celebrated"

LGBTQIA+ identities are beautiful and valid. We provide affirming support for gender exploration, coming out, dealing with discrimination, and celebrating who you truly are.

Holistic Support

"Supporting you means supporting all of you"

Whether you're managing chronic conditions like Parkinson's, supporting a loved one as a carer, or dealing with complex mental health challenges, we see and support all aspects of your life.

What We Offer

Individual Psychological Therapy
