Telehealth Only Practice

We are a telehealth-only practice, which means all sessions are conducted online via secure video connection. We do not have a physical office for in-person appointments.

This allows us to:

  • Serve clients across all of Australia
  • Provide a more comfortable, accessible environment
  • Eliminate travel barriers and waiting room stress
  • Offer flexible scheduling options

Before Your First Session

What to Prepare

Documents

  • Mental Health Care Plan (if you have one)
  • GP referral
  • Any relevant medical reports
  • List of current medications

Technology

  • Test your camera and microphone
  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Have a backup phone nearby
  • Close unnecessary programs

Environment

  • Find a private, quiet space
  • Have water and tissues nearby
  • Comfortable seating
  • Any comfort items you need

Questions to Consider

  • What brought you to seek therapy?
  • What would you like to achieve?
  • Any concerns about starting therapy?
  • What has/hasn't worked in the past?

Need Immediate Support?

If you're in crisis or need immediate support, please reach out to:

  • Lifeline: 13 11 14 (24/7)
  • Suicide Call Back Service: 1300 659 467
  • QLife (LGBTQIA+ support): 1800 184 527
  • 1800RESPECT (sexual assault/family violence): 1800 737 732
  • Kids Helpline: 1800 55 1800
  • Emergency: 000

Please note: Our email and contact form are not monitored for crisis situations. If you're in immediate danger, please contact emergency services.
