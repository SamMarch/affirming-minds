Our Services

Evidence-based, affirming psychological support tailored to your unique needs

Individual Psychological Therapy

$210 per session

Compassionate, evidence-based psychological therapy delivered via secure telehealth. Each 50-minute session is tailored to your unique needs, drawing from a range of therapeutic approaches to find what works best for you.

Medicare Rebate Available: With a Mental Health Care Plan from your GP, you can receive a rebate of $96.65 per session, making your out-of-pocket cost $113.35.

What We Can Work On Together:

→ Anxiety and worry management
→ Depression and low mood
→ Trauma processing and recovery
→ Life transitions and adjustments
→ Relationship challenges
→ Identity exploration
→ Stress and burnout
→ Grief and loss

Therapeutic Approaches:

We integrate evidence-based approaches including Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT) skills, and somatic approaches – all adapted to be neuro-affirming and culturally responsive.

Neurodivergent Support Services

$210 per session

Specialized support for Autistic and ADHD individuals, designed by someone who truly understands the neurodivergent experience. Whether you're newly diagnosed, self-identified, or exploring your neurotype, this is a safe space to be authentically yourself.

Note: We provide post-diagnosis support and exploration, not diagnostic assessments. We can help you understand your neurotype and develop strategies that work with your brain, not against it.

Areas of Support:

→ Post-diagnosis processing and identity integration
→ Understanding and reducing masking
→ Sensory needs and regulation strategies
→ Executive function support
→ Autistic/ADHD burnout recovery
→ Self-advocacy skills
→ Communication and social navigation
→ Managing internalized ableism

LGBTQIA+ Affirmative Therapy

$210 per session

Genuine affirmative therapy from someone within the LGBTQIA+ community. This isn't just "friendly" therapy – it's therapy that deeply understands the unique joys and challenges of LGBTQIA+ life, including the intersection with neurodivergence.

→ Gender identity exploration and affirmation
→ Coming out support at any life stage
→ Navigating family and relationship dynamics
→ Minority stress and discrimination
→ Internalized homophobia/transphobia
→ Intersection of LGBTQIA+ and neurodivergent identities

Safe and Affirming: Use whatever name and pronouns feel right for you. We can also provide support letters for gender-affirming care when appropriate.

NDIS Psychological Services

$230 per session

Capacity-building psychological support for NDIS participants. We focus on developing practical skills and strategies to help you achieve your goals and increase your independence.

NDIS Funding: These services can be funded through your NDIS plan under Capacity Building - Improved Daily Living or Improved Relationships categories.

Support Areas:

→ Emotional regulation and coping strategies
→ Social skills and relationship building
→ Independence and life skills
→ Managing anxiety and mood
→ Building self-advocacy skills
→ Navigating change and transitions

Note: You cannot use NDIS and Medicare funding for the same session. We'll help you understand which funding source is most appropriate for your needs.

Professional Development & Supervision

$210 per hour

Supporting other professionals to provide truly affirming care. Whether you're a psychologist, counsellor, or allied health professional, we offer supervision, mentoring, and training on neurodivergent-affirming and LGBTQIA+ affirmative practices.

Services Include:

→ Clinical supervision for provisional psychologists
→ Professional mentoring and consultation
→ Training on neuro-affirming practices
→ LGBTQIA+ cultural competency development
→ Workplace presentations and workshops
→ Case consultation and support

Specialised Support Programs

$210-$230 per session

Drawing on extensive experience in specialized settings, we offer targeted support for specific populations and conditions.

Parkinson's & Parkinson's Plus Syndromes

Specialized psychological support for individuals and families affected by Parkinson's disease and related conditions. We understand the unique psychological challenges that come with movement disorders.

Carer Support

Dedicated support for carers managing their own wellbeing while caring for others. We address carer burnout, stress management, and maintaining your own identity alongside caring responsibilities.

Complex Mental Health

Support for individuals with complex presentations including personality disorders, complex trauma, and co-occurring conditions. We take a compassionate, non-judgmental approach to complexity.

Veterans Services

Specialized support for veterans dealing with transition to civilian life, trauma, and service-related mental health challenges. DVA funding may be available.
